What is Equilibre.io?

A Portfolio Balancer for Stellar

Equilibre.io is a portfolio balancer for Stellar. In other words, it is a software which helps you to spread your funds across various currencies and crypto assets.

I initially created it to protect my funds against a possible bear market. As it proved reliable, I decided to make it publicly available.

Equilibre.io is merely a sophisticated front-end for the Stellar Decentralized exchange: your account, holdings & operations live on the blockchain.

It works a bit like a multi-currency bank account. Except there's no bank. And you are the only one in control.

A Risk Management Solution

Equilibre.io can help you to implement what is known as a "balanced investment strategy". Basically, the idea is that balancing funds over multiple assets will average a portfolio performance & mitigate the risks.

Here's how it works: we choose a number of assets we want to be exposed to, and we attribute a share of the portfolio to each of them (e.g: 20% each). Then, we "rebalance" the portfolio on a regular basis. This means that we pass trades that bring back each asset to its allocated share.

It is important to understand that Equilibre.io is not an automated trading bot, nor a magic-free-money scheme. It is a product made for people who can decide on their own strategy & who understand the risks they are taking.

A Fancy Account Viewer

Equilibre.io offers a live view of the latest blockchain state. It is also in sync with the latest market data & displays values and historical data in your national currency.

It is one of the few applications (if not the only one) that can retrieves the complete history of any Stellar account, and pack that data into a fancy chart.

A Sophisticated Trading Engine

Equilibre.io is, first of all, a collection of fine-tuned trading algorithms. Since a year, I've crash-tested them in every way I could imagine and packed what worked the best in an easy-to-use interface.

It can transform templates such as "I want 50% of my portfolio in BTC and 50% in XLM" into a transaction that passes the required orders. Forget about trust lines, order books and price picking: all that stuff is dealt with in the background.

The trading engine has a defensive behavior: it prioritizes not losing money over taking risks to make a profit.

A New Security Standard for the Years to Come

Equilibre.io strictly enforce cryptography good practices: I certainly don't want to know about your private key, nor to obtain any kind of control over your account. “Who can protect a house full of gold and jewels?”

When you want to rebalance your portfolio, Equilibre.io generates a transaction request that you can sign using your own wallet. This is possible thanks to protocols known as CosmicLink & SEP-0007.

Those protocols turn any compatible Stellar wallet into something like Metamask for Ethereum. At that time, only a few services are compatibles. However, a number of wallets announced their intention to support transactions requests in the future, including Keybase, Lobster, and Stargazer.

A Free Software, as in Freedom

I think that the free software cooperative mindset can go a long way in improving a project such as Stellar. That's why I released Equilibre.io in the public domain. Here are some of the benefits of it:

Auditability: The software can be proven to contain no malware and no tracker. It can be audited for flaws & proven safe.

Forkability: Any feature implemented by the software becomes de facto available to every other developer. For example, the code I wrote to support Ledger Wallets is freely available to all.
